About Us

Our Story

Strategic Consult (PTY) LTD is a Gaborone-based consulting firm with over 20 years of experience in delivering professional business and financial solutions. Established to support businesses in navigating financial and operational challenges, the firm has built a strong reputation for reliability and professionalism.

We provide a wide range of services including business consulting, accounting, bookkeeping, taxation, and company secretarial services. Our approach focuses on practical, results-driven solutions that help businesses operate efficiently, remain compliant, and achieve sustainable growth.

Over the years, Strategic Consult has supported startups, SMEs, and established organizations across Botswana, delivering services at the right time, cost, and professional standard.
