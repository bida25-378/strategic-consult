Tax planning is an important part of running a successful business. For small businesses, proper tax planning helps reduce financial risk, improve compliance, and avoid unnecessary penalties.

Effective tax planning helps businesses manage cash flow, prepare for tax deadlines, and identify possible savings. It also ensures that businesses are not caught unprepared when tax payments or submissions are due.
